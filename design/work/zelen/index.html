independent flower shopbrand identity2026
the task

A new flower shop needed a mark that works as small as a ribbon tag and as large as a storefront awning, in a category where nearly every competitor reaches for the same soft pastel bouquet.

the solution

The identity centers on a running deer carrying a single flower — open enough to read as gift, giver, or the shop itself — paired with an olive-and-plum palette that skips the expected pink. The system extends across signage, packaging, and a hanging illuminated sign.
