SOUL

lingerie brandbranding, packaging design2023
the task

SOUL needed a premium logo and packaging that could talk about self-love and body positivity in a category that usually treats it with either clinical distance or overt spectacle.

the solution

A quiet serif wordmark carries the premium register, while a woven snake motif — echoing the brand's initial and read as a symbol of strength — extends into a tissue-lined unboxing moment designed to be heard and felt, not just seen.
