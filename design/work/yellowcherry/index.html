YellowCherry

musical duobranding & logo design2024
the task

A musical duo needed a mark as inseparable and energetic as their stage performances, without falling back on generic band-merch typography.

the solution

A continuous, single-line script ties every letter together, echoing the duo's physical closeness on stage. Magenta and yellow carry the mark across print, apparel, and framed prints without needing a secondary palette.
