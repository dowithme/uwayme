independent furniture restorerbrand identity, furniture restoration2024
the task

A one-person furniture restoration business needed an identity with real weight behind it — the client's late artist friend had left him a Suprematist-style painting of a chair, and the brand needed to honor that rather than simply illustrate furniture.

the solution

Rather than redrawing chairs, the identity translates the painting's angular geometry and perspective into the wordmark and a rounded-square icon — a modernist system that reads as considered and handmade at once. A companion film, "The Ritual," extends the brand: it opens in a cold, fluorescent-lit office and resolves into a warm home interior, repositioning the chair from function to comfort.
